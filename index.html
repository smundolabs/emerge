Who We Are

We Don't Give Up on Anyone

eMerge is a Maryland-based organization supporting individuals with developmental, physical and mental health disabilities. Today we serve more than 450 people, many of them rejected by other agencies.

We encourage individuals entrusted to the eMerge family to choose personally enriching paths to living and working in their communities.

Emerge recognizes that services will change, and families will want services that support their family members in remaining part of their family, and change as the needs of each customer change.
