Program

Residential Services

Services are offered for 1 to 3 people living in a home provided and operated by Emerge. The staff intensity level is based up on the needs of each individual.


Supported Living Services


These services are provided in the customer's home, or a home that the individual leases, sometimes with the help of Emerge. These services range from minimal to intense services. Services are built around the individual, and are based on helping them be part of their community.


Personal Support Services


Personal Support Services are provided to a customer in their home or their family's home. These are typically services for a few hours a week, are available any day of the week, and are built around individual needs of each customer. Examples include working with a customer for a few hours in the evening, helping a customer get ready in the morning, or providing support to customers on weekends.

Program

Employment Services

Emerge provides vocational and job training services at Emerge locations to help individuals who would like to get a job in the community, and develop skills needed to obtain paid employment with other organizations.


Mobile Work Crews


Emerge provides employment on various mobile work crews including landscaping, horticultural opportunities, janitorial and grounds maintenance to provide paid employment opportunities for persons with disabilities not yet ready to get a job with another company. Customers in this problem group both earn a wage for work performed and receive training to further their skills.

Supported Employment, Job Development and Coaching


Emerge assists customers who would like to work to get a job with any business in the community with the process of exploring available job options, applying for jobs, and providing job coaching services on the job to assist the individual.
